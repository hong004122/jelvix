DDT Case Studies

We work towards the success of our clients. See how other companies employed the dedicated development team model, reached business goals, and scaled their technology capacity.

  • Auchan

    Goal: building custom web platform for retail in-store experience digitizing

    Our team was assigned to build an online system where customers could place pre-orders for a streamlined in-store experience on a very tight schedule. A key challenge was to prevent overcrowding in Auchan stores during busy periods - before national holidays or special offers, for instance.

    Jelvix crafted PHP-based web platform, stable to high load, proceeding with big data, and storing them with the help of AWS cloud service, with exquisite and thoughtful design, easy-to-use on every mobile device. This project engaged customers that enjoyed the experience in 23 Auchan stores. Over 3 days event 992 orders were placed, and an uplift in sales was recorded (NDA on stat).

    Vista Research Group

    Goal: to optimize monitoring patients’ medical treatment progress.

    Our cooperation started with the outsourcing model. Yet, having proved our effectiveness, we moved to DDT format.

    We have transformed the product from an admin panel with uploading files feature into a complex medical CRM with different roles, chats, programs, centers, uploading and analyzing files features available.

    We always aim to save our clients' time and automate processes. As a result, the product is a success among users and investors, and is prosperous today.

    Viral Vet

    Goal: to create a social network for vets in order to share their experience, stories and treatment methods.

    We started working on a fixed price basis. After releasing the first version of the product, we have continued updating development using DDT model. We continue developing the product further, implementing client’s requirements while bringing our own ideas to the table and immediately reacting to users’ feedbacks.

    As a result, our client has received a qualitative product, well-developed process of communicating with developers, quick adaptation (thanks to operative reacting to governing feedbacks), stability and boost in company’s brand recognition.

    ZEISS

    Goal: enterprise software development for Optics and Optoelectronics industry

    Our team provided development of ZEISS’s partner and customer multi-device and multi-language portal. We were responsible for rolling out software that would integrate specific workflows from each group, connect to the company web-SSO, be simple to use, and interface with third-party applications and SAP.

    Jelvix DDT produced a portal with Web-SSO, APIs, messaging, workflow management, statistics and reports, a product catalog, and Microsoft Cloud Azure. As a result, the client improved communication and coordination, opened new markets for partners, improved brand image and loyalty, and increased sales and cost optimization.
